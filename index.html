How to Play

To play PieClicker all you need to do is click on the large pie. When you have enough pies buy some upgrades so you can make even more profit. The process repeats itself. It is really quite fun.

PieClicker: Empires

The PieClicker Empires update adds tons of new content to the game!
In this update we changed prices and price multipliers for almost every single item in the game.
We also added music disk textures for different songs!
We updated the purchase animations and added golden pie animations.
You can also now sort upgrades by the things they will change!
The ground work for multiplier features was set in place this update.
This is the first update where there are upgrade purchase requirements other than if you can afford it or not!
We made an upgrade that can raise the changes of a golden pie.
We also made a few game performance improvements!

We hope you enjoy this update.
