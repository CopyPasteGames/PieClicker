How to Play

To play PieClicker all you need to do is click on the large pie. When you have enough pies buy some upgrades so you can make even more profit. The process repeats itself. It is really quite fun.

How to Read the Numbers

Reading the numbers in PieClicker can get hard. Even more so when you get really far in the game and there are new units that you haven't seen before. Never fear, we have a helpful chart for you!
Name Symbol Value
Thousand K 10^3
Million M 10^6
Billion B 10^9
Trillion t 10^12
Quadrillion q 10^15
Quintillion Q 10^18
Sextillion s 10^21

Known Issues/Bugs

-If you have a saved price then price changes won't work.
-If you have a saved multiplier and a new update comes out it will use the old multiplier instead of the current one.
